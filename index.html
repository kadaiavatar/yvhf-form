Rules & consent

Please read all rules carefully before agreeing

  • YV&HF does not have an extension policy due to Travel, Injury, or Exam. Extension only provided in case of religious fasting.
  • Players will not be allowed to play in any other batch timings except their own selected batches. Management/Academy or coach is not responsible for any injury caused or any aggravation.
  • In case of any casualty or health problem while playing tennis please consult your Doctor/Physician before starting tennis.
  • All payments must be made in whole prior to the terms by due date. Fees after due date are accepted with a penalty of Rs. 50 per day.
  • In case you have had any serious sickness-illness injury, please declare it before hand. Even in such cases, the management/Academy or coach will not be responsible for re-occurrence or aggravation of the same.
  • Players are responsible for any breakage, damage or loss of the article of YV&HF. They will be penalized for the same.
  • Management is not responsible for any lost or theft of valuable things.
  • You should wear proper tennis shoes which have non-marking out sole.
  • There may be dates where you will be asked to perform specialized tasks or complete along with or without supervision.
  • We accept payment through CASH/CHEQUE/UPI.
  • Any date joining, month will count from date to date.
  • The duration of the batch will contain fitness, theory and other useful contents & breaks over and above in court playing.
  • The management can change the batch structure, batch timing and coaches without prior notice.
  • Permission there may be cancellation of batch for 10-12 days in quarter owing to tournament play, maintenance, etc.
  • Management reserves the right to admission.
  • Physical training given at the academy is only an introduction to fitness programmed. Aspiring competitive players will need to get specialized training.
  • No eatables and harmful substance are allowed in around of court premises.
  • The management has a right cancel the admission without assigning any reason.
  • All players should obey above given all rules and regulation.
